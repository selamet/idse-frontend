Unit 1

Telefon numarası sorma

  • [?] What is your phone number?
  • [+] My phone number is 0(‘zero’, ‘ow’) five double three one nine eight zero five three.

Tanışma cümleleri:

  • İsim sorma:

    [?] Can you tell me your name?
      [-] My name is Selamet. What's yours?
      Note: What's yours yerine `and you`veya `what about you` kullanılabilir.

  • Yaş sorma:

    What is your age?
      Can you tell me your age?
      Could you tell me your age?
      I want to learn your age?
          Cevaplar:
          I am 23 years old.
          My age is 23.

  • It is a pleasure to meet you -> seninle tanışmak bir zevkti.

  • I am sorry for you loss -> Kaybın için üzgünüm (Baş sağlığı)

  • Kısalmalar:

    pto -> p stands for please , t stands for turn, o stands for over.

  • Telefon numarası:

    - Bana ... numaradan ulaşabilirsin:
          - You can call me at 05331968053
          - You can call me from 05.....
          - You can reach(ulaşmak) to me from 05......
          - San nasıl ulaşabilirim?
          - How i reach you.
          - How can i call you.
          - how can i get into contact with you? -> Seninle nasıl iletişim kurabilirim?
          - Give me a number to reach by phone
          - Give me a number to reach withphone

  • Kelime:

    Title -> Başlık (Gazetede ise 'headline')
      line -> şerit, satır, yol
      letter -> harf
      word -> kelime
      sentece -> cümle
      Phrose | promt -> kalıp

  • Ulus sorma:

    What is your nationality?
          - I am turkish.
          - I am a turk.

  • Do you mean -> bunu mu demek istedin.

  • Günler:

    Weekday -> Monday to Friday
      Weekned -> Saturday and Sunday

      Monday -> Pazartesi
      Tuesday -> Salı
      Wednesday -> Çarşamba
      Thursday -> Perşembe
      Friday -> Cuma
      Saturday -> Cumartesi
      Sunday -> Pazar

  • Gün sorma:

    - What is the date today?
      - Tell me the date?
      - Can you tell  me the date?
      - Could you tell me the date?
      Cevaplar:
      - Today is monday.
      - It is monday.
